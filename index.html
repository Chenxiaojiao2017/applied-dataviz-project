CELEBRATING THE 140TH ANNIVERSARY OF CHINESE STUDENTS AT MIT

In 1877, the first student from China matriculated at MIT. By 1910, China was sending more students to MIT than any other foreign country. From these beginnings, the Institute became one of the most popular destinations for Chinese overseas students, especially those seeking to contribute to their country’s modernization through engineering, science and commerce.
